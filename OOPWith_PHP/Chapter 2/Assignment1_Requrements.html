Assignment 1 Requirements


- Create a Web form that allows a user to enter
  their first name, age, and how many hours they
  sleep at night

- Use a constructor on the class you create
  to hold the functions and variables

- Specify the number of hours in a day in a constant

- When they click a "Calcuate" button, use an
  Object-Oriented PHP function to calcuate how many
  years of their life they have slept and then
  dislay a message in the folowing format

  "Hey, Steve you have slept 19 years of your life away!"
